1.Purpose

This document records the results of User Acceptance Testing (UAT) performed on the Choccoladd website prior to and following its production deployment. The objective of UAT was to verify that the delivered website meets the agreed functional and visual requirements, performs correctly across devices and browsers, and is ready for public use under the client's official domain, www.choccoladd.com.

2.Scope of Testing

The following areas of the website were covered during UAT:

  • Homepage — all sections (Hero, Our Story, Company Profile, Products, Gift Sets, In-Store Exclusive, Location, Contact, Footer)
  • Product catalogue pages — All Products, Gift Sets, and Non-Food (Wellness & Skincare)
  • Search, sorting, and sticky navigation behaviour on catalogue pages
  • WhatsApp ordering flow for all products and gift sets
  • Contact form submission and delivery
  • Location map with interactive markers and directions
  • Mobile responsiveness (tested at 375px – 430px widths) and desktop layouts
  • SEO readiness — metadata, Open Graph previews, sitemap, robots.txt, structured data
  • Domain, HTTPS, and canonical URL configuration

All 42 test cases passed and all defects identified during the UAT period have been resolved and verified in the production environment. The website meets the agreed requirements in functionality, design, responsiveness, and search-engine readiness.

Recommendation: The Choccoladd website is accepted for full production use at https://www.choccoladd.com.
